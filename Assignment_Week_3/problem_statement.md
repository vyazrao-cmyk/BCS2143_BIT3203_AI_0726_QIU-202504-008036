## Working project title4

Smart Campus Parking Assistant


## Target users

Students, lecturers, and visitors searching for parking on campus.


## Evidence

Finding parking on campus is a common problem for students, lecturers, and visitors, especially during busy hours. Many people spend several minutes driving around just to find an empty parking space, which causes traffic congestion and wastes time. Studies have shown that smart parking systems can reduce parking search time and improve traffic flow, making campus transportation more efficient.
Reference: Moradkhany, A., Yi, P., Shatnawi, I., & Xu, K. (2019). Minimizing Parking Search Time on Urban University Campuses through Proactive Class Assignment. Transportation Research Record.


## Urgency and social value

Solving this problem reduces congestion, saves time, lowers fuel consumption, and improves the campus experience.
